About PosturePro

Empowering your fitness journey with cutting-edge AI technology and real-time posture analysis

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to professional-grade fitness guidance. PosturePro was founded to bridge the gap between personal fitness and expert coaching, using advanced computer vision to provide real-time form analysis and personalized feedback.

What We Do

Our AI-powered platform analyzes your exercise form through video processing, providing instant feedback to help you perfect your technique, prevent injuries, and maximize your workout effectiveness.

Key Features

  • Real-time pose detection using advanced MediaPipe technology
  • Support for multiple exercises: squats, push-ups, planks, and lunges
  • Detailed form analysis with actionable feedback
  • Video processing with pose overlays
  • Mobile-responsive design for training anywhere
  • Secure user authentication and progress tracking
